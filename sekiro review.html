Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is a game that challenges you to master the art of the blade in a brutal and beautiful world of feudal Japan. You play as Wolf, a one-armed shinobi who is sworn to protect a young lord with a mysterious bloodline. Along the way, you will face deadly enemies, including the fearsome Ashina clan and their leader, Genichiro.

The game is developed by FromSoftware, the creators of the Dark Souls series and Bloodborne, and it shows in their signature style of challenging and rewarding gameplay. Sekiro is not a game for the faint of heart, as you will die many times before you learn how to overcome each obstacle. Another aspect of Sekiro that stands out is the story and the setting. The game is set in the late 1500s Sengoku period, a time of war and chaos in Japan.
